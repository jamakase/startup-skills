# Product Hunt Launch

A complete framework for launching on Product Hunt, based on strategies from Leo Bosuener (60+ #1 launches) and official guidance from Product Hunt CEO Rajiv Ayyangar.

## Should You Launch on Product Hunt?

**Product Hunt is right for you if:**
- Want to catch eye of investors/press
- Need short-term growth boost
- Want genuine feedback from tech enthusiasts
- SEO benefit from high-authority backlink

**Expected Results:**
| Product Type | Signups (Great Success) |
|--------------|-------------------------|
| B2B | 50-300 |
| B2C Productivity | 500-1,500 |
| B2C Wide Appeal | 1,000-3,000 |

**Time Investment:** 50-120 hours preparation (or ~10 hours with professional help)

---

## Phase 1: T-30 Days (Audience Warmup)

### Critical Warning

> **Accounts created on launch day get their votes REMOVED by the algorithm.**

### Tasks

- [ ] Announce upcoming launch to community
- [ ] Ask supporters to create PH accounts NOW (30+ days before)
- [ ] Engage with Maker Network (comment on other launches)
- [ ] Identify high-value users (active PH members with following)
- [ ] Build global strategy (supporters in all time zones)
- [ ] Segment email list by time zone

### Outreach Approach

1. **Personal DMs** to network (highest response)
2. **Email** to existing users
3. **LinkedIn posts** to ICP
4. **Twitter/X** for public awareness

## Phase 2: Day Selection

| Day | Traffic | Competition | Best For |
|-----|---------|-------------|----------|
| Monday | Medium | Low | Higher rank |
| Tuesday | High | High | Maximum traffic |
| Wednesday | High | High | Maximum traffic |
| Thursday | High | High | Maximum traffic |
| Friday | Medium | Low | Higher rank |
| Saturday | Low | Lowest | **Best chance at #1** |
| Sunday | Low | Lowest | **Best chance at #1** |

**Recommendations:**
- Brand awareness/credibility → Monday or Friday
- Maximum traffic → Tuesday/Wednesday/Thursday
- Best shot at #1 spot → Saturday or Sunday

## Phase 3: Launch Assets

### 1. Tagline (Most Important)

**Requirements:**
- Include action verb
- Be clear, not grand
- Evoke curiosity

**Examples:**
- "Make Chrome faster and use 10x less memory"
- "Find the best SEO tools for the job"
- "Launch your own swag store in 3 easy steps"

**Common Failure:** Trying to sound grander but losing clarity.

### 2. Gallery Images (Minimum 3)

- **Show the UI** - PH community wants to see what it looks like
- Embed captions explaining value
- High resolution
- Showcase different facets

### 3. Video (3-5 minutes)

- Simple Loom walkthrough often works best
- Avoid over-polished ($10K videos rarely worth it)
- YouTube link (not private)
- Don't use links that go live at launch (tech issues)

### 4. Maker's Comment

**Structure:**
```
[Origin Story]
What pain motivated you to create this?

[Uniqueness]
What makes it different from competitors?

[Mission]
Where is this going?

[Getting Started]
How people can try it

[PH Exclusive]
Discount/free trial for Product Hunt community
```

## Phase 4: Launch Day Timeline

**Launch at 12:01 AM Pacific Time**

| Time (PT) | Action |
|-----------|--------|
| 12:01 AM | Go live, first announcement |
| 12:01 AM | Email blast #1 (Asia/Australia) |
| 7:00 AM | Second push (US comes online) |
| 7:00 AM | Email blast #2 (Europe/US East) |
| 2:00 PM | Third push (maintain momentum) |
| 2:00 PM | Email blast #3 (US West) |
| All day | 2-3 team members respond to comments |
| All day | Monitor performance, adapt real-time |

### Pro Tips

- Add PH embed badge to your website (shows live upvotes)
- Stagger social posts by time zone
- Personal outreach to key supporters
- Batch comment responses in 2-hour intervals

## Phase 5: Algorithm Rules

### What Matters

| Factor | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Upvotes | Velocity & timing, not just count |
| Comments | Quality engagement matters |
| Upvoter Quality | Karma scores of voters |
| Product Quality | Innovative, well-crafted products |

### Things to AVOID

| Trap | Consequence |
|------|-------------|
| Launch parties (same location votes) | Shadow ban |
| Upvoting rings | Algorithm detection |
| New accounts voting on launch day | Votes removed |
| Bots/fake accounts | Account penalty |

### If You See Spam

New users (red balloon icon) commenting → **Report using flag function**

---

## Phase 6: Beyond Upvotes

### Comments & Reviews Matter for Conversion

Many users check reviews before clicking through.

**Good reviews:**
- Specific about what they like
- Mention actual use cases
- From power users

**Bad reviews:**
- One-liners ("Great product!")
- Generic praise

### Strategy

Get your current power users to write detailed reviews. These drive conversion from PH page to your website.

## Launch Checklist

### T-30 Days
- [ ] Announce launch to community
- [ ] Supporters create PH accounts
- [ ] Engage with Maker Network
- [ ] Identify key supporters globally
- [ ] Segment email list by timezone

### T-14 Days
- [ ] Finalize launch day
- [ ] Create tagline (test with users)
- [ ] Prepare gallery images (3+)
- [ ] Record demo video
- [ ] Write maker's comment

### T-7 Days
- [ ] Set up email sequences (by timezone)
- [ ] Draft social posts (staggered)
- [ ] Prepare team assignments
- [ ] Test all links and assets

### T-1 Day
- [ ] Final reminder to supporters
- [ ] Team briefing
- [ ] Set up monitoring dashboard
- [ ] Add PH badge to website

### Launch Day
- [ ] 12:01 AM PT: Go live
- [ ] Execute email sequence
- [ ] Execute social sequence
- [ ] Respond to comments (2-hour batches)
- [ ] Monitor and adapt
- [ ] Celebrate!

### Post-Launch
- [ ] Thank supporters
- [ ] Analyze results
- [ ] Follow up with leads
- [ ] Document learnings
